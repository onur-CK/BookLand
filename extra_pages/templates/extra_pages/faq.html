Frequently Asked Questions

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the United States. International orders may take 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

Creating an account is easy! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the navigation menu, fill out the required information, and you'll be able to track orders, save wishlist items, and enjoy a personalized shopping experience.

We offer a 30-day return policy for most books. Books must be in new, unread condition with no damage. Shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are secure and encrypted to protect your personal information.

The wishlist allows you to save books you're interested in for later. You can add items to your wishlist by clicking the heart icon on any book. These items are saved to your account and can be easily added to your cart when you're ready to purchase.
