Returns and Exchanges

30-Day Return Policy

At BookLand, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy for most books.

Return Conditions

  • Books must be returned within 30 days of purchase
  • Items must be in new, unread condition
  • Original packaging and receipt are required
  • Protective plastic covers must remain intact

Non-Returnable Items

  • Marked books or books with visible wear
  • Special edition or signed books
  • Digital downloads or gift cards
  • Books purchased during clearance sales

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Log into your BookLand account
  2. Navigate to your order history
  3. Select the item you wish to return
  4. Follow the online return request process
  5. Print the provided return shipping label

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, a refund will be processed to your original payment method. Refunds typically take 5-7 business days to appear on your statement.

Exchanges

If you received a damaged or incorrect book, we'll gladly exchange it. Contact our customer support team, and we'll guide you through the process.

Need Help? Contact our customer support at support@bookland.com or call +123 456-789 for assistance with returns.
